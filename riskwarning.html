This Risk Warning provides important information regarding the risks associated with using the Calfes platform and interacting with digital assets.
By accessing or using the platform, you acknowledge that you have been informed of these risks. This document should be read alongside the Terms of Use.

1. General Risk Disclosure

Digital assets, including cryptocurrencies, are highly volatile and involve significant risk. Their value may fluctuate rapidly, and users may lose some or all of their funds.
Calfes does not guarantee the value, performance or liquidity of any digital asset and does not guarantee profits or returns.

2. Non-Custodial Risk

Calfes operates as a non-custodial wallet. This means:
- Users retain sole control over their private keys and recovery phrases
- Calfes does not store or have access to user credentials
If you lose your private keys or forget your recovery phrase, or if you disclose them to any third party, your assets may be permanently lost and cannot be recovered by Calfes.

3. Market Risk

Digital asset markets may be subject to:
- Significant price volatility
- Periods of low liquidity
- Market manipulation or irregular trading activity
Prices may change rapidly and unpredictably.

4. Calfes Token – Liquidity Risk

At the time of launch, the CALFES Token may not have an active secondary market or liquidity.
There is no guarantee that trading markets or liquidity will be established at any particular time or at all. As a result, users may not be able to exchange the token for other digital assets or fiat currency.
Any future introduction of trading or liquidity is subject to technical, operational, and regulatory considerations.

Use of blockchain-based applications involves technical risks, including:

Transactions executed on blockchain networks are generally irreversible.

Users are responsible for verifying all transaction details prior to submission.
